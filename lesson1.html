Installing Hard Drives

  • Part 1: Creating a Safe Environment ( For You and the PC )

    • Clean and organize work area
    • Turn off computer and disconnect power cord
    • Protect yourself from electrostatic discharge

  • Part 2: Installing the Drive

    • Decide which bay will hold the drive
    • Slide drive in and secure one side with two screws
    • Connect power cable to hard drive
    • Connect SATA data cable
    • Check all connections and power up the system
