Nelly's Bio

Hi, welcome to my page!

My name is Nelida Hernandez Solis and I am a proud Year Up Trainee as well as one of the code anchors for this Module 3 project. I am 19 years old as well as the lead in education in my family. I have two brothers and I am the middle child.

Finding out about Year Up has been one of the greatest opportunities I have encountered. I am a few weeks away from internship and am anxious to see where I can apply my skills. Information Technology was not an area of interest until I was exposed to the fundamentals of creating a website, which captivated my attention. I was chosen for a Udacity scholarship program. In this program, I will be creating pages to earn a Nanodegree in Front-end Web development. As I continue with my Year Up experience I am becoming very passionate about coding and about closing the Opportunity Divide. The Opportunity Divide consists of the gap between talented young adults seeking for opportunities and corporate companies seeking for motivated employees. How does Year Up contribute? Well, Year Up is that branch that connects both ends to grow that beautiful flower, which in this case is the start-up of a successful career.
