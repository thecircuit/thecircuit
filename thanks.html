About Year Up

Year Up is an organization that " empowers low-income young adults to go from poverty to professional careers in a single year." Year Up Chicago focuses specifically on IT (Information Technology). Because we believe in YearUp's mission to close the opportunity we have made this website not only to teach others' but to showcase our skills. We can do our part to close the opportunity divide by showcasing our skills and showing that talent doesn't always look a certain way. Then we will also make it easier for others after us to get into corporate America.

Thank you for coming to our site!
