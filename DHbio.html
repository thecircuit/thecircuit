Donovan's Bio

After running into financial difficulty I applied at YearUp. At YearUp I have learned various skills in hardware, software, and Coding. I still play chess now, and I have started to learn how to play Shogi and Go. I have enjoyed playing video games since I was three years old. My favorite game is Super Mario Bros. 3. Now I mostly play on my Playstation 3, or on the go with my Nintendo 3DS.
