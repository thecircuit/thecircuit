How to Properly Use Conjunctions

What is a conjunction and what are the types of conjunctions?

A conjunction links independent and dependent clauses together. There are three types of conjunctions.

Coordinating Conjunctions: For, and, nor, but, or, yet, so. Also known as FANBOYS.
